Wedding to be! Britney Spears has commented on the rumors about her marriage

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  241 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Britney announced the good news on social networks the other day: she and her fiancé Sam Asghari will soon become parents. The next day, the pop diva shocked fans with yet another confession by calling Sam “husband” in a published post.

The words of the star were perceived unequivocally – the singer’s fans decided that the lovers registered their marriage during the holidays in Mexico. However, a source close to the couple denied this information.

An insider told HollywoodLife that the couple won’t get married until after the birth of their first child together: “Britney and Sam have been calling each other ‘husband’ and ‘wife’ since they got engaged, but they’re not officially married and want to wait until the baby is born. So they will have nine months to plan the celebration, but Britney is completely focused on the pregnancy.”

This pregnancy is the third and highly anticipated for the pop star. Under the guardianship of her father, Britney was forbidden to have children, and she was forced to take contraceptives constantly. Recall that in 2004, Britney Spears married dancer Kevin Federline, and three years later, the couple broke up. The ex-spouses have two sons: 16-year-old Sean and 15-year-old Jayden. “Britney’s kids are very excited to hear that their mom is expecting a baby. She told them first, knowing that it would greatly affect their lives. The baby will have wonderful brothers, Britney believes,” added a HollywoodLife source.
