Us Weekly and Pretty Little Liars have partnered up again to bring you a sneak peek at each week’s new episode with behind-the-scenes photos. Scroll down for more on this week’s pics, and check back every Tuesday morning to see new ones.

Mona’s locked up, and you know what happens when that happens!

In these sneak-peek photos from tonight’s Pretty Little Liars‘ episode (titled “O Brother, Where Art Thou”), it looks like Mona (Janel Parrish) is still grounded (while occasionally managing to escape to labs, of course). But the Liars, including Hanna (Ashley Benson) and Spencer (Troian Bellisario), are giving the teen fiend some visitors, and she looks a little shock-eyed about the whole thing.

While we don’t have too much scoop on tonight’s plot—besides that, like winter, Charles is coming—we do have a behind-the-scenes look at the PLL actors getting themselves ready to shoot. Check out this child’s-eye view of Mona’s bedroom, sans roof! (The better to soundstage, my dears.)
