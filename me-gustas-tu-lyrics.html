Me Gustas Tu is an infectious and vibrant song by the renowned musician Manu Chao that instantly captivates listeners with its irresistible rhythm and catchy melodies.

Released in 2001, this iconic track showcases Chao’s unique fusion of Latin, reggae, and world music, transporting listeners on a sonic journey filled with joy, love, and a celebration of life.
