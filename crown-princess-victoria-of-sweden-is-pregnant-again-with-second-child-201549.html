Crown Princess Victoria of Sweden Is Pregnant Again With Second Child

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  173 words  |  Martina Birk

Congratulations are in order! Crown Princess Victoria of Sweden and her husband, Prince Daniel, are expecting baby No. 2, the palace announced on their official Facebook page on Friday, Sept. 4.

“They Royal Highnesses Crown Princess Victoria and Prince Daniel are happy to announce that The Crown Princess is expecting their second child,” the post reads. “The birth is expected to take place in March of 2016.”

According to the announcement, there will be no changes to the pair’s public schedule through the rest of the year.

The royal couple are already parents to Princess Estelle Silvia, 3, who was named for her maternal and paternal grandmothers.
