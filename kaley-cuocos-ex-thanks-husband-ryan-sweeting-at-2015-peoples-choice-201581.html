Kaley Cuoco's Ex Thanks Husband Ryan Sweeting at 2015 People's Choice

 Posted on June 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  368 words  |  Larita Shotwell

It’s handy to have ex boyfriends in high places! Kaley Cuoco forgot to thank her husband, Ryan Sweeting, during her acceptance speech for Favorite Comedic TV Actress at the 2015 People’s Choice Awards. Her solution? Get her ex-boyfriend and costar Johnny Galecki to do it for her!

Not long after Cuoco, 29, accepted her first award, the happy actress returned to the stage with her entire Big Bang Theory cast to accept another award for Favorite TV Show.

This time it was Galecki, 39, (who plays Dr. Leonard Hofstadter in the hit comedy series) who got to do the talking. But despite being Cuoco’s ex-lover, the actor proved he was still on great terms with his costar, by using the opportunity to make up for his ex’s forgetful faux pas.

“Hey, just real quick, I’d like to take a brief moment, because Kaley forgot during her speech to thank her husband, Ryan,” Galecki said, kind of awkwardly, while Cuoco gave him a thankful pat on the back.

“You are my heart, and I can’t imagine what I’d be without you,” Galecki then joked before going on to thank fans more seriously for their support and love for the show.

Cuoco and Galecki dated for two years in secret back in 2007. But while Galecki has been tight-lipped about their hidden relationship, he did admit this time last year, that he and his former love had managed to maintain a strong friendship.

“We’re dear friends, still,” Galecki told CBS Watch! Magazine in December 2013. “Kaley’s not just an ex, she’s a part of my life.”

Galecki went on to attend Cuoco’s wedding to tennis hunk Sweeting, and according to Cuoco, there were no hard feelings.

“He was in the front row at my wedding, with a huge smile on his face,” Cuoco told Cosmopolitan magazine in April. “It took a minute, a bit of awkwardness,” she admitted, of the transitioning process from boyfriend and girlfriend to best pals. “By the grace of God, we became best buddies and moved on in such an elegant way. [He’s] one of my closest friends.”
